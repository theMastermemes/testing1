# Races of the Realms

The world is populated by diverse peoples, each with their own cultures, abilities, and histories. From the ancient [Valdari](/docs/races/valdari) to the mysterious [Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin), each race brings unique perspectives and capabilities to the realms.

## Major Races

### Humanoid Races
- **[Valdari](/docs/races/valdari)** - Noble guardians of ancient knowledge
- **[Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn)** - Master craftspeople of the mountains
- **[Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin)** - Enigmatic dwellers of twilight

### Other Peoples
- Forest spirits and elemental beings
- Dragonblooded lineages
- Planar travelers and their descendants

## Racial Relations

The relationships between races shape much of the political and social landscape. See [Regions](/docs/regions) for how different races have settled across the world.
