# Ironborn

Masters of forge and mountain, the Ironborn are renowned throughout the realms for their unmatched craftsmanship and their deep connection to the earth itself.

## Racial Traits

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Size** | Medium |
| **Speed** | 25 feet |
| **Lifespan** | 250-350 years |
| **Languages** | Common, Dwarvish, Terran |

## Physical Description

Ironborn are shorter but broader than most humanoids, typically 4-5 feet tall with powerful builds. Their skin often has a metallic sheen, and their hair ranges from copper to iron-grey. Many sport elaborate braids woven with small metal ornaments.

## Culture and Society

Ironborn society revolves around **Forge Clans** - extended families that specialize in different aspects of craftsmanship. Honor is earned through skill, and the finest artisans are revered as living legends.

### Major Clans
- Clan Steelstrike (weapons and armor)
- Clan Goldweaver (jewelry and fine arts)  
- Clan Stonebreaker (architecture and engineering)

## Abilities and Traditions

The Ironborn have natural resistance to fire and toxic metals. Their **Forge-Song** tradition allows master crafters to imbue items with minor magical properties through ritual chanting during creation.

## Relations with Other Races

- **[Valdari](/docs/races/valdari)**: Ancient trade agreements for rare materials
- **[Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin)**: Mutual wariness, but respect for each other's skills
- **Surface dwellers**: Primary suppliers of worked goods and weapons

## Ironborn Territories

The great mountain fortress of **Khaz Ankor** serves as their cultural capital. See [Regions - Mountain Holds](/docs/regions) for more details.
