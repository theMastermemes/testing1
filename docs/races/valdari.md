# Valdari

The Valdari are an ancient race known for their deep connection to mystical knowledge and their role as guardians of sacred sites throughout the realms.

## Racial Traits

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Size** | Medium |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |
| **Lifespan** | 300-400 years |
| **Languages** | Common, Valdaran, Ancient Script |

## Physical Description

Valdari are tall and graceful, typically standing 6-7 feet in height. Their skin has a subtle luminescent quality, and their eyes often reflect starlight. Most have elongated ears and naturally silver or pale gold hair.

## Culture and Society

The Valdari organize themselves around **Circles of Knowledge** - scholarly communities that preserve different aspects of ancient wisdom. They are natural diplomats and often serve as mediators between other races.

### Notable Circles
- Circle of Stars (astronomy and divination)
- Circle of Memory (history and lore)
- Circle of Harmony (diplomacy and peace-keeping)

## Relations with Other Races

- **[Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn)**: Long-standing trade partnerships
- **[Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin)**: Cautious respect, shared interest in ancient mysteries
- **Humans**: Patient teachers and advisors
