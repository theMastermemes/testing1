# Arms, Armor, and Magical Items

From simple tools to legendary artifacts, equipment plays a crucial role in the lives of adventurers and common folk alike.

## Weapon Categories

### Martial Weapons
- **Valdari Moonblades**: Elegant weapons that glow with inner light
- **Ironborn Runic Axes**: Dwarven weapons inscribed with protective runes
- **Shadowkin Whisper Blades**: Silent weapons that seem to drink in light

### Ranged Weapons
- **Ironborn Crossbows**: Precision-crafted with superior range
- **Valdari Starbows**: Bows that fire arrows of pure energy
- **Traditional bows**: Various woods and construction styles

## Armor Types

### Heavy Armor
- **Ironborn Plate**: Master-crafted full plate with integrated runes
- **Wardkeeper Aegis**: Blessed armor worn by protector orders
- **Scale mail**: Flexible protection from various creatures

### Light Armor
- **Shadowkin Leathers**: Armor designed for stealth and mobility
- **Studded leather**: Common protection for scouts and rogues
- **Mage robes**: Cloth armor enhanced for spellcasters

## Magical Items

### Common Magic Items
- **Light stones**: Rocks that glow on command
- **Message scrolls**: Single-use communication spells
- **Healing potions**: Basic restorative magic

### Rare Artifacts
- **The Codex Eternal**: Book containing all [spell](/docs/spells) knowledge
- **Aegis of the First Ward**: Shield used by the original [Wardkeepers](/docs/classes/wardkeeper)
- **The Shadow Crown**: Artifact of the [Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin) ruling house

## Crafting and Enhancement

Master crafters can enhance equipment through:
- **Runic inscription**: [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn) technique for permanent bonuses
- **Blessed consecration**: Divine enhancement for holy warriors
- **Arcane infusion**: Magical enhancement through spell-weaving
