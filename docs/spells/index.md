# Spells and Magic

Magic permeates the realms, manifesting through various schools of study and natural phenomena. From the protective wards of the [Wardkeepers](/docs/classes/wardkeeper) to the shadow magic of the [Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin), magical power takes many forms.

## Schools of Magic

### Traditional Schools
- **Abjuration**: Protective and defensive magic
- **Divination**: Information-gathering and foresight
- **Evocation**: Direct magical force and energy
- **Enchantment**: Mind-affecting and charm magic
- **Transmutation**: Change and transformation
- **Conjuration**: Summoning and creation

### Realm-Specific Arts
- **[Ward Magic](/docs/spells/ward-magic)**: Protective barriers and shields
- **[Shadow Weaving](/docs/spells/shadow-weaving)**: Manipulation of darkness and stealth
- **[Forge Magic](/docs/spells/forge-magic)**: Enhancement of crafted items

## Magic Sources

Different practitioners draw power from various sources:
- **Arcane**: Study and understanding of magical theory
- **Divine**: Faith and connection to deities
- **Natural**: Harmony with elemental forces
- **Ancestral**: Wisdom and power of ancient spirits

## Spell Components and Casting

Most spells require a combination of:
- **Verbal**: Spoken incantations or words of power
- **Somatic**: Precise gestures and movements  
- **Material**: Physical components or focuses
- **Concentrative**: Sustained mental effort
