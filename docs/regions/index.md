# Lands and Territories

The realms span vast continents, from the mystical [Valdari](/docs/races/valdari) enclaves to the mighty [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn) mountain holds.

## Major Regions

### The Northern Reaches
- **Khaz Ankor**: The great mountain fortress of the Ironborn
- **Frostvale**: Harsh tundra lands of ancient magic
- **The Singing Peaks**: Mountains where wind magic is strongest

### The Central Kingdoms
- **Goldleaf Valley**: Fertile farmlands and trade centers
- **The Twilight Marches**: Borderlands between light and shadow
- **Academy Isle**: Seat of magical learning

### The Southern Wilds
- **The Endless Forest**: Domain of forest spirits and druids
- **Shadowmere**: Mysterious marshlands claimed by the [Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin)
- **The Glass Desert**: Magical wasteland of crystallized sand

## Places of Power

Scattered throughout the realms are locations where magic runs stronger:
- **Ley line nexuses**: Crossroads of magical energy
- **Ancient ruins**: Remnants of lost civilizations
- **Divine shrines**: Sacred sites blessed by the [deities](/docs/deities)

## Travel and Trade

The **Merchant Roads** connect most major settlements, protected by treaties between the races and patrolled by various [warrior orders](/docs/classes).

## Political Structure

Most regions maintain their own governance, with the **Council of Realms** serving as a diplomatic body for inter-regional issues.
