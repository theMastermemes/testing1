# Gods and Divine Beings

The pantheon of the realms includes ancient gods, ascended mortals, and powerful entities that shape the spiritual landscape.

## The Prime Pantheon

### Greater Deities
- **Aethon the Lightbringer**: God of sun, truth, and justice
- **Nyx the Veilkeeper**: Goddess of night, secrets, and the space between
- **Terros the Worldshaper**: God of earth, craftsmanship, and endurance

### Lesser Deities
- **Sylvana**: Goddess of forests and natural growth
- **Ferrox**: God of smiths and metalwork (popular among [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn))
- **Whisper**: Deity of knowledge and hidden truths (revered by [Lorekeepers](/docs/classes/lorekeepers))

## Divine Domains

Each deity grants power to their faithful through specific domains:
- **Light**: Healing, protection, and revelation
- **Shadow**: Stealth, secrets, and necessary darkness  
- **Forge**: Craftsmanship, endurance, and creation
- **Nature**: Growth, animals, and natural balance
- **Knowledge**: Learning, memory, and wisdom

## Worship and Temples

Different [races](/docs/races) favor different deities:
- [Valdari](/docs/races/valdari) often worship Aethon and Whisper
- [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn) revere Terros and Ferrox
- [Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin) serve Nyx exclusively

## Divine Magic

Clerics, paladins, and other divine casters draw their power from these deities, with their abilities reflecting their patron's nature and domains.

## Ascended Mortals

Legendary heroes who achieved divine status through great deeds:
- **Saint Valdris**: [Valdari](/docs/races/valdari) lorekeeper who catalogued all magical knowledge
- **The Ironheart**: [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn) smith who forged weapons to defeat an ancient evil
