# Wardkeeper

Wardkeepers are the stalwart guardians who stand between civilization and chaos, blending martial discipline with protective magic to shield others from harm.

## Class Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Primary Role** | Tank/Support |
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Primary Abilities** | Constitution, Wisdom |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Constitution, Wisdom |
| **Armor Proficiencies** | All armor, shields |

## Core Features

### Ward Magic
Wardkeepers channel protective energies to create magical barriers, heal wounds, and enhance their allies' defenses. Their magic draws from ancient oaths and the collective will to protect.

### Guardian Stance
At higher levels, Wardkeepers can enter specialized stances that provide different tactical advantages:
- **Aegis Stance**: Enhanced damage reduction and spell resistance
- **Sanctuary Stance**: Area protection for nearby allies
- **Retribution Stance**: Reflects damage back to attackers

### Oath Traditions
- **Oath of the Hearth**: Protectors of communities and innocent folk
- **Oath of the Threshold**: Guardians against otherworldly threats  
- **Oath of the Wild**: Defenders of natural places and creatures

## Equipment and Fighting Style

Wardkeepers favor heavy armor and shields, often wielding one-handed weapons that allow them to maintain defensive postures. Many carry blessed items or family heirlooms that enhance their protective abilities.

## Notable Wardkeepers

- **Theron Brightshield**: [Valdari](/docs/races/valdari) defender of the Sacred Grove
- **Mira Stoneward**: [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn) guardian of the Mountain Gates
