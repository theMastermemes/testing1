# Lorekeepers

Scholar-mages who preserve and wield the accumulated wisdom of ages, Lorekeepers transform knowledge itself into magical power.

## Class Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Primary Role** | Caster/Support |
| **Hit Die** | d6 |
| **Primary Abilities** | Intelligence, Wisdom |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Intelligence, Wisdom |
| **Skills** | Arcana, History, Investigation, plus two others |

## Core Features

### Knowledge Domains
Lorekeepers specialize in different spheres of learning, each granting unique magical abilities:

- **Chronicler**: History, divination, and time magic
- **Naturalist**: Understanding of creatures, plants, and natural phenomena  
- **Theorist**: Pure magical theory and metamagic techniques
- **Antiquarian**: Ancient languages, lost civilizations, and artifact lore

### Memory Palace
Advanced Lorekeepers develop mental constructs that allow them to store vast amounts of information and recall it with perfect clarity. This grants them bonus spells and enhanced magical abilities.

### Shared Knowledge
Lorekeepers can temporarily grant their knowledge to allies, allowing fighters to know exactly where to strike or helping scouts identify dangers by sharing relevant lore.

## Spellcasting

Lorekeepers prepare spells by consulting their tomes, scrolls, and notes. They excel at:
- Divination and information-gathering magic
- Utility spells that solve problems through knowledge
- Rituals that require extensive research and preparation

## Tools of the Trade

Every Lorekeeper maintains:
- **Grimoire**: Their personal spellbook and research notes
- **Research Kit**: Magnifying glass, inks, measuring tools
- **Reference Library**: Portable collection of essential texts

## Famous Lorekeepers

- **Valdris the Chronicler**: [Valdari](/docs/races/valdari) keeper of the Great Library
- **Korgan Ironscribe**: [Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn) expert on ancient metallurgy and forge magic

## Academic Institutions

- The **Collegium Arcanum**: Premier magical academy
- **Circle of Scribes**: Organization dedicated to preserving knowledge
