# Classes and Professions

Whether born to power or trained through dedication, adventurers follow various paths that define their abilities and role in the world.

## Warrior Classes
- **[Wardkeeper](/docs/classes/wardkeeper)** - Protectors who blend martial prowess with defensive magic
- **Voidblade** - Shadow warriors who strike from darkness

## Mystical Classes  
- **[Lorekeepers](/docs/classes/lorekeepers)** - Scholar-mages who wield ancient knowledge
- **Spiritcaller** - Those who commune with elemental and ancestral spirits

## Choosing a Path

Most individuals show aptitude for their calling early in life, though some discover their path later through circumstance or training. See [House Rules](/docs/house-rules) for character creation guidelines.

## Multiclassing and Prestige

Advanced practitioners often blend techniques from multiple disciplines or achieve recognition in prestigious organizations. These paths require dedication and often the approval of existing masters.
