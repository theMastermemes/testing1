# Creatures of the Realms

From the tiniest sprite to ancient dragons, the realms teem with life both mundane and magical. This bestiary catalogues the creatures adventurers might encounter.

## Categories

### By Intelligence
- **Beasts**: Natural animals with basic instincts
- **Monstrosities**: Unnatural creatures, often magically created
- **Humanoids**: Intelligent tool-users and civilizers
- **Fey**: Creatures of magic and nature
- **Fiends**: Evil outsiders from dark planes
- **Celestials**: Good outsiders from bright planes

### By Habitat
- **[Forest Dwellers](/docs/bestiary/forest-spirits)**: Creatures of wood and glade
- **Mountain Guardians**: Beings of stone and peak
- **Shadow Realm Entities**: Things that lurk in darkness

### By Danger Level
- **Harmless**: Creatures that pose no threat
- **Caution**: Potentially dangerous if provoked
- **Hostile**: Actively aggressive to intruders
- **Lethal**: Extreme danger to experienced adventurers

## Creature Relationships

Many creatures have complex relationships with the [civilized races](/docs/races):

- **Domesticated**: Kept as pets, mounts, or livestock
- **Symbiotic**: Mutually beneficial relationships
- **Neutral**: Neither helpful nor harmful
- **Predatory**: View civilized folk as food
- **Territorial**: Defend their domains aggressively

## Magical Creatures

Some creatures are inherently magical, possessing:
- **Spell-like abilities**: Innate magical powers
- **Magic resistance**: Natural protection against spells
- **Magical components**: Body parts useful in spell crafting
