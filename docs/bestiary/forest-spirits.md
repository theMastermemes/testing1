# Forest Spirits

Ancient guardians of woodland realms, forest spirits embody the living essence of trees, streams, and glades.

## Creature Profile

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Size** | Small to Large |
| **Type** | Fey |
| **Challenge** | 1/4 to 8 |
| **Environment** | Temperate and tropical forests |
| **Languages** | Sylvan, Common (when willing) |

## Varieties

### Dryads
Tree-bound spirits who rarely venture far from their oak homes. Dryads are generally peaceful but fiercely protective of their groves.

**Abilities:**
- Tree stride (teleport between trees)
- Charm person (natural enchantment)
- Fey ancestry (resistance to enchantment)

### Sprites
Tiny winged spirits that serve as forest messengers and scouts. They delight in playing harmless pranks on travelers.

**Abilities:**
- Invisibility at will
- Detect thoughts (surface emotions)
- Poison needles (sleep-inducing)

### Grove Wardens
Larger spirits that patrol vast forest territories. They appear as amalgamations of various forest creatures and plant matter.

**Abilities:**
- Animate trees and plants
- Control weather (localized)
- Regeneration while in forest

## Behavior and Society

Forest spirits organize around **Sacred Groves** - ancient trees or clearings that serve as focal points for their communities. They have complex relationships with:

- **[Valdari](/docs/races/valdari)**: Ancient pacts of mutual respect
- **Druids and rangers**: Often serve as guides and allies
- **Loggers and farmers**: Territorial conflicts are common

## Interaction Guidelines

### Peaceful Contact
- Approach sacred sites with reverence
- Offer gifts of spring water or flower wreaths
- Speak in Sylvan if possible
- Avoid carrying iron weapons openly

### Warning Signs
- Sudden silence in the forest
- Trees that seem to watch or move
- Paths that lead in circles
- Unusual animal behavior

## Related Creatures

- **[Treants](/docs/bestiary)**: The eldest and most powerful forest guardians
- **Wild Hunt**: Fey that ride with the seasonal spirits
- **Corrupted Spirits**: Blighted versions found in tainted forests

## Magical Properties

Forest spirit components are valued for:
- **Nature magic**: Enhancing spells that affect plants and weather
- **Enchantment**: Creating items that charm or beguile
- **Healing potions**: Their essence can cure natural poisons
