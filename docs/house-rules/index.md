# Campaign Rules and Modifications

These are the specific rules modifications and additions used in our Codex of Realms campaigns.

## Character Creation

### Racial Selection
When creating a character, consider how your chosen [race](/docs/races) fits into the world:
- **[Valdari](/docs/races/valdari)**: Start with additional language proficiencies
- **[Ironborn](/docs/races/ironborn)**: Gain proficiency with smith's tools
- **[Shadowkin](/docs/races/shadowkin)**: Start with one stealth-related skill

### Class Modifications
- **[Wardkeepers](/docs/classes/wardkeeper)**: Use Wisdom for spell attack rolls
- **[Lorekeepers](/docs/classes/lorekeepers)**: Can prepare additional spells equal to Int modifier

## Magic Rules

### Spell Components
- Material components under 50gp are assumed to be in a component pouch
- Expensive components must be acquired through roleplay or purchase
- Some [regional](/docs/regions) areas have restrictions on certain magic types

### Rest and Recovery
- Short rest: 1 hour of light activity
- Long rest: 8 hours with at least 6 hours of sleep
- Rest interruption rules apply for dangerous environments

## Combat Modifications

### Initiative
- Group initiative: All PCs act on the same count
- Players decide action order each round
- Encourages teamwork and tactical planning

### Healing and Death
- Death saves reset on long rest
- Healing magic leaves subtle scars (roleplay element)
- Resurrection magic requires rare components

## Social and Exploration

### Languages
- Common is the trade language
- Regional dialects exist in remote areas
- Ancient languages require special training to learn

### Travel
- Use the [regional](/docs/regions) travel times and encounter tables
- Weather and season affect travel speed
- Some areas require special preparation or guides

## Experience and Advancement

### Milestone Leveling
- Characters advance at story milestones
- Major discoveries, resolved conflicts, and completed arcs grant levels
- Encourages roleplay over combat optimization
