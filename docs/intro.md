# Welcome to the Codex of Realms

Welcome, traveler, to the **Codex of Realms** - your comprehensive guide to the mystical lands, creatures, and magics of our world.

## What You'll Find Here

This codex contains knowledge of:

- **[Races](/docs/races)** - The diverse peoples that inhabit these realms
- **[Classes](/docs/classes)** - Paths of power and profession
- **[Spells](/docs/spells)** - Arcane arts and divine miracles  
- **[Bestiary](/docs/bestiary)** - Creatures both mundane and magical
- **[Regions](/docs/regions)** - Lands, cities, and territories
- **[Deities](/docs/deities)** - Gods, goddesses, and divine beings
- **[Equipment](/docs/equipment)** - Weapons, armor, and magical items
- **[House Rules](/docs/house-rules)** - Campaign-specific modifications

Use the navigation bar above to explore different sections, or use the [search feature](/search) to find specific information quickly.
